The 4-4-4 Pattern

🫁
Breathe in for 4 counts — feel your belly rise
⏸️
Hold gently for 4 counts — feel steady
💨
Breathe out for 4 counts — let it all go

💜 You can't do this wrong. Even just breathing a little slower helps your brain feel calmer. Your body is smart — it listens to your breath.
