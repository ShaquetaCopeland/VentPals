Meet the Flock

Each of these birds was born from a feeling — something you've probably carried too. These are your VentPals. They don’t just listen — they fly with you.

Clover

Flies for comfort. A gentle soul who shows up when your heart feels heavy. Clover listens with warmth and never rushes your feelings.

Breezy

Flies for bravery. Breezy encourages you to speak your truth, even when your voice shakes. You’ve got this — Breezy’s flying beside you.
