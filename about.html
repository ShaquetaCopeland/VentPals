Meet VentPals

Where storms are shared, not silenced.

Our Mission

VentPals is a soft landing for heavy hearts. We believe in the healing power of being heard — no fixing, no judgment, just connection. Whether you're a parent juggling chaos, someone navigating identity, or a soul healing from heartbreak — this is a space made for you.

Who We Are

Founded by Shaqueta "Kweet" Norman, a mother, creator, and survivor, VentPals was born out of real-life pain, perseverance, and a longing for safe emotional community. After navigating divorce, distance from her kids, and mental health challenges, she decided no one should ever feel alone in their silent battles.

“She’s the kind of person who carries the weight of storms and still shows up with sunshine for others.”
