What is VentPals?

VentPals is a gentle, anonymous space where people come to let things out, connect with kind strangers, and find their flock — a small group of like-minded listeners who get it. We believe everyone deserves a place to land when life feels heavy.

Why Birds?

Birds migrate, they nest, they sing, and they heal — just like us. Each flock represents a different emotional pattern or strength. Your bird isn't just cute; it’s your companion through growth and recovery.

How it Works

  • You join anonymously and complete a soft intake guided by Pebble.
  • You’re matched to a flock based on your personality and patterns.
  • You chat, connect, earn badges, grow levels, and help others heal too.
