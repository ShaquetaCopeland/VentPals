Staying Safe 🔒

Before you begin, let's go over a few important rules about your privacy.

🙈
Your feelings are private.

Only you see what you write — unless you choose to share it with someone you trust.

🚫
No strangers, ever.

VentPals has no public posts. No one can find you, message you, or join your circle unless you invite them.

👨‍👩‍👧
Parents help keep you safe.

A parent or guardian will link to your account. They can see who you've shared with — but only if you choose to share.

💜
You are always in control.

You choose who to talk to, what to share, and when. VentPals never forces you to share anything.

🆘
If something feels wrong, tell a trusted adult.

VentPals is here to help, but it's not a crisis service. If you ever feel unsafe, please talk to a trusted grown-up straight away.
