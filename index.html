What is VentPals?

VentPals is your soft place to land — an anonymous, emotionally safe space where you can talk freely, be heard, and begin healing. Whether you're carrying grief, stress, or just need a moment to breathe, there's a bird in the flock for you.

Why We Exist

We believe that everyone deserves a space to be understood. No judgment. No pressure. Just people (and birds) who get it.

How It Works

You choose your topic, your bird, or let Pebble guide you. Join rooms, connect with others, or fly solo with gentle prompts and AI-powered reflection tools.

Who Created VentPals?

Shaqueta Norman — founder, dreamer, and storm-survivor — built VentPals after realizing how powerful it can be to simply be heard. Her mission is to bring soft support to those carrying heavy things.
