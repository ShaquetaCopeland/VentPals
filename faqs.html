VentPals — Parent FAQ

What is VentPals?

VentPals is a child‑friendly emotional support platform that helps kids understand their feelings, express themselves safely, and build emotional independence.

Is VentPals therapy?

No. VentPals is not therapy and does not replace mental‑health care. It’s an emotional learning tool designed to help kids build healthy emotional habits.

Is VentPals safe for my child?

Yes. VentPals includes a required privacy module, no public posting, no strangers, no ads, and parent‑controlled Circles.

What information does VentPals collect?

Only the minimum needed to operate the platform. Children’s entries stay private unless they choose to share them.

Can I see what my child writes?

Only if your child chooses to share it with you. VentPals encourages healthy communication while respecting a child’s emotional autonomy.

How does VentPals teach privacy?

Kids complete a short, interactive Privacy & Safe‑Sharing Module before using any tools.

What ages is VentPals for?

VentPals is designed for children ages 6–12.

What if my child shares something concerning?

VentPals guides children toward trusted adults and provides tools like the Ask‑With‑Courage Letter Generator.

Does VentPals replace talking to my child?

No. VentPals strengthens communication by giving kids the words and confidence to start conversations.

How much does VentPals cost?

The core experience is free. Optional keepsakes and parent tools support the nonprofit mission.

Why does VentPals use characters?

Children learn best through play. Characters help them feel safe, engaged, and understood.

How do I get started?

Create a parent account, set up your Circle, connect your child, and review safety settings.

VentPals — Kid FAQ

What is VentPals?

VentPals is a cozy place where you can learn about your feelings and use fun tools to help your heart feel calmer and stronger.

Do I have to talk about my feelings?

Nope! You get to choose what you want to do. VentPals never rushes you.

Who can see what I write?

Only the people you choose. Your feelings stay private unless you decide to share them.

What’s a Circle?

Your Circle is your group of trusted grown‑ups who can help you when you want support.

What if I don’t know how to explain my feelings?

VentPals has tools and characters that help you find the right words.

What if I feel something really big?

VentPals helps you choose a trusted grown‑up and even write a letter if you want.

Is VentPals a game?

Not exactly — but it is fun! You’ll meet friendly characters and explore calming tools.

Why do I have to learn about privacy?

Because your feelings are important, and you deserve to stay safe online.

Can I use VentPals every day?

Yes! You can visit anytime.

What if I make a mistake?

VentPals is a no‑judgment zone. You can always try again.
