🔒 Our Safety Model — Why VentPals Is Built This Way

Children

Username + PIN. No email. Privacy first. Children control their own emotional space.

Parents

Email + password. Max 2 per child. Linked with a one-time child-generated code.

Trusted Adults

Teachers, counsellors, coaches. Only linked through parent approval. Child initiates.

Every Link Is Explicit

Who, what role, who approved, and whether it's active or revoked — always visible.

VentPals is designed for children. Email accounts are often shared, monitored, or created by adults. Using email would give adults too much control over a child's emotional space.

Instead, children use:

  • A username they choose
  • A PIN they can remember
  • Parent approval for account activation

This keeps the child's identity private and safe.

Children need to choose their bird, their name, their avatar, and understand privacy before they enter the emotional world. Onboarding is part of the emotional education, not just account creation. It ensures children arrive as informed participants, not passive users.

Your approval confirms this is your child, you understand the platform, you support their emotional growth, and you are aware of who they may connect with. It also prevents strangers from creating fake child accounts.

Children deserve connection with the adults who care for them. VentPals supports two parents, guardians, blended families, and separated or divorced parents. The child chooses who they talk to. No parent can remove the other without the child seeing that change. This protects the child from emotional gatekeeping.

Yes — but only with your approval. Your child requests the link, you receive it and approve or deny it. If approved, that adult can support your child inside VentPals. Your child chooses who they trust. You ensure those adults are safe.

Parent Codes are child-specific and one-time use. This prevents adults from claiming children who aren't theirs, accessing unrelated kids, or guessing codes. Only the child who generated the code can be linked to you.

VentPals is built around emotional safety, not adult conflict. Children deserve transparency, stability, and connection with both parents when safe. Removing a parent secretly can harm the child emotionally. VentPals prevents that — not to interfere with adult relationships, but to protect the child.
